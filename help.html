⚡ Electricity

How do I optimize electricity usage? +

You can optimize electricity usage by:

  • Shifting heavy equipment to off-peak hours (2-5 AM)
  • Using the "Try Optimizer" feature on the Electricity page
  • Monitoring peak usage times and adjusting schedules accordingly
  • Letting AI auto-schedule based on real-time demand
💡 Pro Tip: The AI automatically shifts non-essential loads to off-peak hours when electricity prices are lowest.
⚡ Go to Electricity
How does AI schedule electricity based on temperature? +

The AI uses real-time temperature data to optimize electricity usage:

  • When temperature is high: AI pre-cools the building during off-peak hours (2-4 AM) to reduce daytime AC load
  • When temperature is moderate: AI reduces cooling and shifts to natural ventilation
  • When temperature drops: AI adjusts heating schedules to maintain comfort while saving energy
✅ Result: Up to 25% reduction in HVAC electricity costs with AI temperature-based scheduling.
⚡ Try Temperature Optimizer
What is the recommended electricity usage target? +

The recommended target varies by time of day:

  • Off-peak (12 AM - 6 AM): 2.0-2.5 kWh
  • Mid-peak (6 AM - 12 PM): 3.0-3.5 kWh
  • Peak hours (12 PM - 8 PM): 3.5-4.2 kWh
  • Evening (8 PM - 12 AM): 2.5-3.0 kWh
⚡ Target: Keep usage below 4.2 kWh during peak hours to avoid the danger zone.

💧 Water

How can I reduce water consumption? +

To reduce water consumption effectively:

  • Monitor water usage metrics on the Water page in real-time
  • Schedule irrigation during early morning (5-7 AM) to reduce evaporation
  • Fix leaks promptly using our automated leak detection system
  • Enable rainwater harvesting for irrigation and non-potable uses
  • Set pool circulation to off-peak hours (after 10 PM)
💧 Saving: Proper scheduling can reduce water consumption by up to 30%.
💧 Go to Water
How does the AI help with water scheduling? +

The AI optimizes water usage through smart scheduling:

  • Peak hour detection: AI shifts water-intensive tasks to off-peak hours
  • Rainwater harvesting: When rain is detected, AI switches irrigation to rainwater supply
  • Leak detection: AI monitors flow patterns and alerts you to potential leaks
  • Pool circulation: AI schedules pool pumps during cheapest electricity rates
🌧️ Smart Feature: During rainy days, the AI automatically uses 100% rainwater for irrigation!
💧 Try Water Optimizer
How do I add a sensor to the monitoring system? +

Adding a new sensor is easy:

  • Go to Monitoring page from the sidebar
  • Click the "Add New Sensor" button at the top
  • Select the sensor type from the dropdown (Light, Motion, Temperature, etc.)
  • Enter a sensor name and location
  • Specify the unit of measurement (e.g., lux, °C, dB)
  • Click "Add Sensor" to activate it
📡 Tip: New sensors appear immediately in the dashboard and start sending data in real-time.
🔍 Go to Monitoring
