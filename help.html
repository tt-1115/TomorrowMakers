❓ Frequently Asked Questions

⚡ Electricity

How do I optimize electricity usage? +

You can optimize electricity usage by:

  • Shifting heavy equipment to off-peak hours (2-5 AM)
  • Using the "Optimize Now" feature on the Electricity page
  • Monitoring peak usage times and adjusting schedules accordingly
  • Implementing AI-powered scheduling recommendations
What is the recommended electricity usage target? +

The recommended target varies by time of day:

  • Off-peak (12 AM - 6 AM): 2.0-2.5 kWh
  • Mid-peak (6 AM - 12 PM): 3.0-3.5 kWh
  • Peak hours (12 PM - 8 PM): 3.5-4.2 kWh
  • Overall daily target: 3.2 kWh average
How does the AI optimization work? +

Our AI optimization analyzes your usage patterns and:

  • Identifies peak consumption periods
  • Suggests optimal scheduling changes
  • Predicts potential savings
  • Provides real-time recommendations

💧 Water

How can I reduce water consumption? +

To reduce water consumption:

  • Adjust irrigation to early morning (5-7 AM)
  • Fix leaks promptly using our detection system
  • Use water-efficient fixtures
  • Monitor usage patterns and adjust schedules
What is considered optimal water usage? +

Optimal water usage targets:

  • Daily average: 0.8 m³ per day
  • Peak hours (10 AM - 2 PM): 0.9-1.1 m³
  • Off-peak hours: 0.5-0.7 m³
  • Monthly target: ~24 m³

👷 Manpower

How do I improve manpower utilization? +

To improve manpower utilization:

  • Redistribute tasks evenly across shifts
  • Use the "Optimize Now" feature
  • Monitor peak utilization hours
  • Balance workload between morning and evening shifts
What is the ideal utilization rate? +

Ideal utilization targets:

  • Target utilization: 85%
  • Peak hours: 80-90%
  • Off-peak hours: 60-75%
  • Weekly overtime: Less than 4 hours

📊 Reports & Analytics

How do I generate a report? +

To generate a report:

  • Navigate to the Reports page
  • Select Daily, Monthly, or Yearly view
  • Click "Generate Full Report"
  • Download the PDF automatically
What data is included in the reports? +

Reports include:

  • Electricity usage patterns and trends
  • Water consumption analysis
  • Manpower utilization metrics
  • AI-powered optimization recommendations
  • Projected savings and impact analysis
